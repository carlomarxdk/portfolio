About me

Third-year PhD student at the Technical University of Denmark (Section for Cognitive Systems), a member of the Social Complexity Lab and Copenhagen Center for Social Data Science at the University of Copenhagen.

My research primarily explores the Representation of Human Behavior (part of the Nation-Scale Social Networks project). I am curious about the development of tools that allow us to get deeper insights into the evolution of one's life as well as the spread of ideas in communities. Currently, I explore the capabilities of Transformer-based models to analyze sequences of socio-economic data, encompassing aspects such as labour, health, and education. the supervision of Professor Sune Lehmann and Professor Lars Kai Hansen.

From September 2022 until February 2023, I visited Professor Tina Eliassi-Rad at the Network Science Institute (Northeastern University) in Boston, US. Under her supervision, I explored the area of the Algorithmic Auditing and Fairness.
